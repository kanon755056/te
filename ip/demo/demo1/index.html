WebRTC Demo Portal
This demo shows a simple browser to browser audio-only or audio-video chat scenario step by step.
  1. Click the audio_video.html link to open the web app in a new page.
  2. Click the audio_video.html link to open the same web app in another page.
  3. Treat one web app as Caller and the other as Callee, follow the steps on the pages to make a call.
